# 📊 Loan MIS & Business Performance Analysis (SAS)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on building a **Loan MIS (Management Information System)** to track and analyze business performance in a loan portfolio. It simulates real-world reporting used in financial institutions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Develop daily and monthly MIS reports  
- Track key business KPIs and SLA metrics  
- Analyze performance across branches, products, and channels  
- Automate reporting processes for efficiency  

---

## 🧾 Dataset Description
The dataset contains loan-level information including:
- Application, Approval, and Disbursement Dates  
- Loan Amount  
- Loan Status (Approved, Rejected, Disbursed, Pending)  
- Branch and State  
- Product Type (Home Loan, Personal Loan, Auto Loan)  
- Sourcing Channel (Direct, DSA, Online)  

---

## ⚙️ Project Workflow

### 1. Data Cleaning & Validation
- Handled missing and inconsistent data  
- Standardized date formats  
- Ensured data accuracy for reporting  

---

### 2. KPI Development
Built key performance indicators to track business health:
- Total Disbursement  
- Loan Volume  
- Turnaround Time (TAT)  
- SLA Compliance  
- Lead Funnel Conversion  

---

### 3. MIS Reporting
Developed structured reports for business monitoring:
- Monthly MIS (Disbursement & Loan Trends)  
- Daily MIS (Operational Tracking)  
- Branch-wise Performance  
- Product and Channel Analysis  

---

### 4. Business Performance Analysis
- Identified high-performing branches and products  
- Analyzed trends in loan disbursement  
- Evaluated conversion rates across loan stages  
- Highlighted operational inefficiencies  

---

### 5. Automation
- Automated MIS generation using SAS macros  
- Reduced manual effort in reporting  
- Enabled repeatable and efficient report creation  

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- Identified top-performing branches contributing maximum disbursement  
- Observed monthly growth trends in loan portfolio  
- Evaluated SLA adherence and delays  
- Analyzed product-wise contribution to overall business  

---

## 🚀 Tools & Technologies
- SAS (Base SAS, PROC SQL, Macros)  
- Microsoft Excel  

---

## 📁 Deliverables
- Monthly MIS Report  
- Branch Performance Report  
- KPI Summary Tables  

---

## 💡 Conclusion
This project demonstrates how MIS reporting and data analysis can support business decision-making in financial services by providing clear visibility into performance and operational efficiency.
